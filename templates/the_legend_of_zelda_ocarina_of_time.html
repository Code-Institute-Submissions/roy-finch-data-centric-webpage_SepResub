The Legend Of Zelda Ocarina Of Time

Initial Opinion

This is a game for the Nintendo 64, and is part of the The Legend of Zelda series. This game took a step in a different direction though and presented Link and his adventures through a new dimension. At first this game can cause a mixture of votes in whether it was a bad or good game but I believe it was a great presentation of a really emersive adventure. It has several amazing maps and a very long run time onces played through. Even though you may not enjoy the collectables the game still has a very long runtime.
